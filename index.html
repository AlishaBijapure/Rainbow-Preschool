Frequently Asked Questions

Do you help with potty training?

Yes, we assist children in the Playgroup and Nursery programs with their potty training journey collaboratively with parents.

What food is provided?

We offer healthy, child-safe, and hygienic snacks tailored to young children's nutritional needs.

What are your timings?

Our school runs from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM for standard programs, with an optional daycare facility until 4:00 PM.
